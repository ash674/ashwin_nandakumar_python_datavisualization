CANADA'S PERFORMANCE IN OLYMPICS

Canada's Performance in Olympics has always been good and in the past decade they have improved drastically. Especially Men's perfomance was constant but women's performance improved rapidly in 21st Century when compared to 19th Century. The Improvement in Women's performance helped canada to reach a solid 50.4% of gold medals in total.

Number of Medals won by men have been steady in 20th Century and they started win a lot of medals at the end 20th Century and at the start of 21th Century.

Number of Medals won by women in 20th Century is really less and just accounts to a total of 34 medals. But the best part of women's performance is the 21st Century where they won more than 50% of total medals

Number of Gold medals by men is highest in 20th Century between the years 1940 - 1960. Then the number of gold medals won went down but reached the peak in 21st Century.

Gold Medals won by Women in 20th Century was always between 0-5 in number. Then their highest number of medals is in 21st Century.

When Number of medals won by Men and women are compared , men dominate women by a whopping 23%. This is because of the performance by men in 20th century. They had a constant performance when compared to women.
